01 — Overview

More than
an English
centre

Currently Active · Language Center

Knowledge Horizon is an English language centre founded by Phan Lê Ngọc Vy, dedicated to helping students, university learners, and working professionals develop genuine communicative competence in English.

Not just the ability to pass exams — but the confidence and capacity to use the language meaningfully in real contexts.

"We do not teach English.
We teach people — who happen
to be learning English."

02 — Philosophy

Language learning should be
rigorous, relevant, and human.

Every course, every class, and every interaction at Knowledge Horizon reflects the same learner-centred philosophy that has guided fifteen years of teaching practice. This is not a centre that follows templates — it is a space where teaching is treated as a professional discipline, and learning is taken seriously.

05 — What Makes Us Different

Research-led.
Human-centred.

Every course at Knowledge Horizon is informed by current research in second language acquisition, cognitive psychology, and reflective teaching practice.

This is not a centre that follows templates. It is a space where teaching is treated as a professional discipline, and where genuine learning — not just test scores — is the measure of success.
